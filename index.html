35A117 — Ramp B  Critical
▶

Scope (Original RRM): Concrete overlay recommended

Scope (Current): Full deck replacement — directed by WVDOH on Nov 5, 2025

Why the change: Jason Bryan raised a safety concern Oct 27 — "material come loose beyond the installed netting." WVDOH Operations Management decided deck replacement is "a better long-term option for the Division."
